---

Billing in ZenStatement centers on **charges** — billing entries that can be one-time or recurring, configured against a customer and plan. Every charge goes through the same four-step creation flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Basic Information">
    Charge name, description, and the customer and plan it belongs to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pricing">
    How the charge amount is calculated. See <a href="/pricing-models/fixed-value">Pricing Models</a> for the available approaches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Billing Cadence">
    The billing frequency for the charge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conditions">
    Which transactions the charge applies to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Every charge — regardless of pricing model — goes through ZenStatement's maker-checker governance flow before it's active: a maker configures it, and a checker reviews and approves before it takes effect.
