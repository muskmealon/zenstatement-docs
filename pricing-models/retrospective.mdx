---
title: "Retrospective"
description: "The charge amount is determined after the billing period, based on actuals."
---

Retrospective pricing defers the rate calculation until after the billing period has ended, based on actual transaction data rather than a rate set in advance. This is useful when the correct rate can only be determined once a full period's activity is known.
