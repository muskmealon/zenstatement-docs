---
title: "Fixed Value"
description: "A flat amount applied to a charge, regardless of usage."
---

Fixed Value is the simplest pricing type: a flat amount is applied to the charge, regardless of usage or transaction volume.

Fixed Value charges can be extended in two ways:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Price Escalation" href="/pricing-models/price-escalation">
    Schedule automatic, governed rate increases over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Price Lookup" href="/pricing-models/price-lookup">
    Apply different fixed rates based on transaction conditions, via a lookup table.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
