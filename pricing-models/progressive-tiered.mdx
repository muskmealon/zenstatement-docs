---
title: "Progressive Tiered"
description: "Tiered pricing where each slab's rate applies only to the volume within that slab."
---

Progressive Tiered pricing also defines rate tiers (From → To → rate), but unlike Flat/Volume Tiered, each tier's rate applies **only to the volume that falls within that slab** — similar to how progressive income tax brackets work.

## Configuration

Configuration follows the same pattern as Flat/Volume Tiered: tier rows, a range basis (Percentage, Unit Price, or Flat Pricing), and an attribute aggregation method (Sum or Count) drawn from transaction file attributes.
